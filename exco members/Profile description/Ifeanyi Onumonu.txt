Ifeanyi Onumonu (Financial Secretary) graduated from UNSSN in 1989. He is a Fellow of Institute of Chartered Accountants of Nigeria. A seasoned Financial Analyst and Project Manager and the Financial Controller of Amen City Ltd, one of the  leading real estate development company. Ifeanyi is a devout Christian and married with three children. A strong advocate for protection and preservation of natural environment. Currently serving as Financial Secretary of UNSSNAA.
