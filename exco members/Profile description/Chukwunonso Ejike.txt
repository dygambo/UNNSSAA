Chukwunonso Ejike (Vice-President) is a Professor of Medical Biochemistry at the Alex Ekwueme Federal University Ndufu-Alike. He enrolled at the UNSSN in 1992 and graduated in 1998. He was Senior Prefect in the 1997/98 session. Beyond science, he is also a creative writer and an essayist. Nonso is married with children. He currently serves as Vice-President of UNSSNAA.
