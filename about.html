The University of Nigeria Nsukka Secondary School Alumni Association (UNNSSAA) is a global network of leaders, thinkers, and change-makers united by our shared formative years in Nsukka.

Who We Are

We are more than just graduates; we are a lifelong community dedicated to the advancement of our alma mater and the professional growth of our members.

Founded on the principles of academic rigor and cultural diversity that define the University of Nigeria Nsukka, our association serves as a bridge between generations. We foster mentorship, create professional synergies, and invest in the future of the school that shaped us.

Leadership

Meet the Exco Members

The association is led by alumni whose work spans finance, public service, engineering, technology, and entrepreneurship.

President

Chioma S. Ndubisi-Etuk

Head of the Subsidiary Management Unit at the NSIA, coordinating portfolio performance and institutional alignment across subsidiary companies. She is also a Board member of NSSF, a former Access Bank professional, and a proud alumna of the University of Nigeria Secondary School, Class of 1988.

Vice-President

Chukwunonso Ejike

Professor of Medical Biochemistry at Alex Ekwueme Federal University Ndufu-Alike. He enrolled at UNSSN in 1992, graduated in 1998, served as Senior Prefect in the 1997/98 session, and is also a creative writer and essayist.

Secretary

Amaka Onuigbo

An environmentalist and entrepreneur with a restaurant and bakery in Abuja. She enrolled at UNSSN in 1996 and graduated in 2002, and she currently serves as the General Secretary of UNSSNAA.

Treasurer

Emeka Anumadu

A business manager with a hair transplant clinic in Lagos. He is of the 1991 set of UNSSN and is also a pensioner with Fidelity Bank and a farmer.

Financial Secretary

Ifeanyi Onumonu

A Fellow of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of Nigeria, seasoned Financial Analyst and Project Manager, and Financial Controller of Amen City Ltd. He graduated from UNSSN in 1989 and currently serves as Financial Secretary of UNNSSAA.

Publicity Secretary

Ofobuike Amankwe

A Principal System Analyst at the University of Nigeria, Nsukka Campus. He enrolled at UNSSN in 1995 and graduated in 2001, studied Fine and Applied Arts at UNN, and is also a writer of folk tales inspiring his animation work.

Assistant Secretary

Shawn Eneje

A registered engineer specialized in construction and renewable energy. A University Secondary School Nsukka alumnus of the Class of 2004, he also consults in climate finance and serves the community as Assistant General Secretary of UNNSSAA.
